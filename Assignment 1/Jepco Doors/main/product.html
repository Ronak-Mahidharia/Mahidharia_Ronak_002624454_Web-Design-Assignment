Laminated Doors

Laminate doors are made from layers of MDF wood, cardboard and resin, before being decorated with colour or texture to create different desired effects.

They’re also very easy for manufacturers to produce so they’re usually cost-effective and offer a good value alternative to traditional timber doors.

Moulded Doors

Molded doors are made of wood by-products and are pressed together in forms to create the particular door style. Molded doors come in two quality levels: hollow core

and solid core. As the names suggest, the hollow core door is a lighter weight door with less sound control than the solid core door.
